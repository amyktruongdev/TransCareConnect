About TCC

We are a group of undergraduate UCLA students striving to mitigate the harm and fear faced by trans youth in their interactions with the medical system.

We were inspired to create TransCareConnect [TCC] because we were challenged in our local hackathon to think about what we could do to distribute health equity.

This was the original brainstorming process for the type of product we wanted to make.

TCC wanted to take the time to acknowledge all of QWERHacks, Fiona, Timid Robot, and Spencer for helping us through our journey of development!!!!
